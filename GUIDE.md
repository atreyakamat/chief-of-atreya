# CHIEF OS v2.0 — USER GUIDE & FEATURE LIST

Welcome to your new Second Brain. CHIEF OS is designed to be a high-end, context-aware intelligence layer for your daily workflow.

---

## 🚀 1. The Core Philosophy
CHIEF doesn't just "chat." It monitors your **active view**, **open browser tabs**, and **incoming notifications** to provide proactive support. Everything is designed to be "Neural," meaning the AI always has the context of what you are working on.

## 🛠️ 2. Key Features

### 📂 Workspaces (Context Siloing)
*   **What it is:** Separate areas for different projects (e.g., "Pixel N Purpose", "Solvix Studios").
*   **How to use:** Create a new Workspace in the sidebar. When a Workspace is active, the AI automatically focuses all "Add Task" commands and summaries to that specific project.

### 📥 Intelligence Inbox
*   **What it is:** A global landing zone for thoughts, mobile notes, and quick actions.
*   **Mobile Sync:** Go to **Settings** to find your **Mobile Ingest URL**. Use this in iOS Shortcuts or Android Tasker to send voice/text notes directly to your Inbox from anywhere in the world.

### 🎤 Voice & Wake Word
*   **What it is:** Hands-free control of your OS.
*   **How to use:** Say **"Hey Zen"** or press **Ctrl + Space** to trigger the neural link. You can dictate tasks or ask for summaries out loud.

### 📸 Photographic Memory
*   **What it is:** AI-powered visual search.
*   **How to use:** Press **Alt + S** at any time. CHIEF will "see" your screen, extract the text, and store it in its neural memory for future retrieval.

---

## ⚡ 3. The Quick Action Bar (No More Commands!)
Located at the top of the Chat Panel, use these buttons for instant intelligence:
1.  **📝 Task:** Automatically drafts a task based on your current chat or view.
2.  **📊 Summary:** Generates a high-level report of your entire day across all Workspaces.
3.  **🎤 Voice:** Manually triggers the microphone for voice-to-text.
4.  **💡 Focus:** Analyzes your open browser tabs and tells you what you should actually be working on right now.

---

## 🧠 4. Brain Selection (Local vs. Cloud)
In the top-right of the Chat Panel, you can toggle your **Neural Engine**:
*   **Ollama (Local):** 100% Private. Runs on your machine. Best for sensitive data.
*   **Groq / NVIDIA / OpenRouter:** Ultra-fast, high-reasoning cloud models. Best for complex coding or deep planning. (Requires API Key in Settings).

---

## ⌨️ 5. Keyboard Shortcuts
*   `Ctrl + Space`: Toggle CHIEF Dashboard.
*   `Alt + S`: Capture Photographic Memory.
*   `Esc`: Close Modals / Exit Onboarding.
