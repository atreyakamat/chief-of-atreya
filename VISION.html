Zen Agent Vision: Your Personal Jarvis

Zen is an autonomous, privacy-first personal assistant designed to run locally on your Ryzen 5700G system with minimal resource footprint.

Core Architecture

  • Wake Word: "Hey Zen" using lightweight openWakeWord.
  • Memory: Single-file SQLite architecture (No complex DB clusters).
  • Capture: Alt+S triggered screenshots with instant OCR/Vision analysis.
  • Efficiency: Optimized to work within 14GB RAM; relies on cloud-based AI providers (Groq/OpenRouter) to keep local CPU usage low.

Integration Roadmap

  • Communication: WhatsApp (Web), Zoho Cliq, LinkedIn, Instagram, Discord.
  • Work & Planning: Google Calendar (Full CRUD), Email (IMAP/Gmail), GitHub.
  • Research & Discovery: Reddit searching & analysis.

Key Philosophy

Autonomy: Zen doesn't just manage; it acts. It drafts replies, schedules events, and organizes your project health by synthesizing data across disparate platforms into a single, unified workflow.
