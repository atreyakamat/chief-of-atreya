🌐

Browser Orchestration

Tracks your Chrome tabs via DevTools Protocol. Detects distractions, monitors focus time, and nudges you back on track.

🔔

Notification Intelligence

Intercepts OS notifications, logs them with timestamps, and uses Claude to classify urgency and summarize your backlog.

⏰

Smart Reminders

Natural language reminder creation. Recurring support, context-aware break nudges, and daily AI briefings at your configured time.

🎙️

Voice Interface

"Hey Chief" wake word detection with local Whisper transcription and text-to-speech responses. Push-to-talk fallback with Ctrl+Space.

🧠

Claude AI Brain

Anthropic's Claude with full tool_use — creates reminders, summarizes notifications, suggests focus strategies with your full context.

🛡️

Privacy First

Everything runs on localhost. Your data stays in a local SQLite database. No cloud sync, no telemetry, no tracking.

How it works

01

Install & Configure

Run setup.bat, add your Anthropic API key to .env, and launch Chrome with remote debugging.

02

Launch CHIEF

Run npm start. The orchestrator boots all modules — browser monitor, notification tracker, voice engine, and Claude.

03

Command Your Day

Use the dashboard or say "Hey Chief" to create reminders, check your tab focus, get notification summaries, and more.

⚡ CHIEF

Built with Node.js, SQLite, Claude AI, and Whisper. Runs entirely on your machine.
